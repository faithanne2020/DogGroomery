+

We have 3 main services that we provide for cats. These include a basic nail trimming, a bath and trimming, and finally a full blowout which includes a nail triming, bath and hair trim. For more information please view our page on Services.

What Services do you provide for dogs?

+

There are 3 main services for dogs. similarly to our cat services we provide a nail trimming, a bath, and a full groom option. for more information on these please check our Services page.

Are there any at home tips for cats?

+

There are many things that you can do for your cat at home that will inprove there hair and nails. One such thing would be to make sure the shampoo you use is safe and ment for cats. Many Human shapmooes are not safe for cats or dogs. For more infromation please check our At Home page where we go into more detail.
